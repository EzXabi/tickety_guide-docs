# Tickety Documentation
<p>Ticket management like __Never__ before</p>

Welcome to Tickety's documentation. Enclosed you will find an ever growing collection of guides and information to help you set up and use our Ticket system bot in your Discord server.

## Who or what is Tickety ?
Started in 2020 Tickety is an extremely powerful yet easy to understand ticket support bot designed to help Discord server owners to deliver help and support to their users.

At Tickety we believe in delivering a highly versatile and yet easy to understand ticket support bot which will give you the power you need at your fingertips to deliver a professional support system without the need to pouring many hours into learning the little nuances.
